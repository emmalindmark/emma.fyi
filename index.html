Portfolio of Projects

Here is a representation of different projects that I have done during my education as well as in my free time.

The images under the category "App project - BaconLogin" is from a project where I, together with a team, developed an app called BeaconLogin. It is an application that together with bluetooth devices called beacons, work as an electronic personnel ledger for companies working in the construction industry. The app is programmed in React Native.

The images under the category "App project - Foodity" is from a project where I, together with a team, developed an app called Foodity. It is an application where restaurants can give away their leftover food to charities who can then give it to people in need. The images are shown from the charity's point of view. The app is programmed in React Native.

The images under the category "Patches" are from when I was a board member in "IT-sektionen" with the post "Graphically responsible". They are made in Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop.
